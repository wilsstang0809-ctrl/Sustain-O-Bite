About Sustain-O-bite

Sustain-O-bite is an environmental organization focused on transforming food waste into eco-enzyme. We believe that small actions at home can lead to meaningful impacts for a more sustainable planet.

What is Eco-Enzyme?

Eco-Friendly

Eco-enzyme is produced through the fermentation of organic waste, helping reduce household food waste.

Multi-Purpose

It can be used as a natural cleaner, liquid fertilizer, and odor neutralizer.

Simple & Accessible

Made from everyday kitchen waste, eco-enzyme is easy for anyone to produce.

How Do You Make Eco-Enzyme?

It's very simple!

Just combine the following ingredients with this ratio:

  • 1 part brown sugar or molasses
  • 3 parts fruit peels and leftover vegetables
  • 10 parts clean water

Place all ingredients into an air-tight plastic container or jar. Store it at room temperature and allow it to ferment. Release gas occasionally during the early fermentation stage. After the fermentation period, your eco-enzyme is ready to be used.
